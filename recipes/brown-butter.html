Brown Butter

Making brown butter might seem difficult (and it can be), but it is a skill worth mastering.

Browned butter adds a nutty and rich flavor to baked goods that is simply irresistible! Your baking will be elevated by mastering this essential technique!

Necessary Ingredients:

  • Salted or unsalted butter. The higher the quality the better!

Directions:

  1. Add room temperature butter to a small saucepan.
  2. Turn the heat on the stove to medium.
  3. Allow butter to melt while sturing constantly. Butter should bubble slightly but should not sputter or boil.
  4. Cook butter until it produces a nutty and rich flavor and has turned a rich goldent color.
  5. Remove from heat quickly (the butter can easily over cook and burn, so be careful).
