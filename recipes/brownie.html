Dark Chocolate Brownies

The best brownies ever!

Everyone will be impressed by this delicious recipe! These tasty treats are as easy to make as they are delectable. This is truly the prefect recipe for any busy lady.

The directions below will walk you through what to do. Do not fear if you are a baking beginner, this recipe is so forgiving it is practically mistake proof!

Necessary Ingredients:

    Wet Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp honey

    • Dry Ingredients:

  • 402 grams of white granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 128 grams of flour
  • 32 grams of dutch processed coco powder
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of dark chocolate chips or chunks
