Dark Chocolate Brownies

The best brownies ever!

Everyone will be impressed by this delicious recipe! These tasty treats are as easy to make as they are delectable. This is truly the prefect recipe for any busy lady.

The directions below will walk you through what to do. Do not fear if you are a baking beginner, this recipe is so forgiving it is practically mistake proof!

Necessary Ingredients:

    Wet Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp honey

    • Dry Ingredients:

  • 402 grams of white granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 128 grams of flour
  • 32 grams of dutch processed coco powder
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of dark chocolate chips or chunks

Directions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt butter until browned. For extended directions on making brown butter, click here!
  3. Add salt,honey, and white granulated sugar to browned butter.
  4. Wisk in eggs to butter and sugar mixture.
  5. In a seperate bowl, sift and wisk the flour and coco powder together.
  6. Mix the wet and dry ingredients together. (Do not over-mix the batter)
  7. Add in the chocolate chips.
  8. Bake in oven for 30 minutes or until center no longer giggles when shaken.
  9. Let cool, cut, and enjoy!
